Reclaiming Urban Spaces

We are a team of five women from diverse backgrounds—architecture, sociology, software engineering, and automotive engineering—united by our passion for technology and data-driven solutions. During this bootcamp, we embarked on a project focused on Mexico City, analyzing population dynamics, access to services, and urban infrastructure to predict patterns of violence. Given Mexico's alarming rates of gender-based violence, uncovering these correlations can provide crucial insights for targeted interventions. Our aim is to contribute findings that inform policies in a city grappling with significant challenges related to gender-based violence. By leveraging our diverse expertise, we strive to foster safer and more inclusive urban environments through innovative data analysis and actionable recommendations.
